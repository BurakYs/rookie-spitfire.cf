FAQ

How do I login to the bot?

1. Use the "/login" command.
2. Wait for the bot to reply with a message that only you can see.
3. Click the button in the message.
4. You will be redirected to Epic Games, after website loads you will be asked to authorize. Click "Confirm".
5. Get back to Discord and wait 1-2 seconds then you will see a message that you have successfully logged in.

How can I trust you not to steal my account?

Obviously, it is up to you to trust us. I don't have any proof to convince you, but we want you to know this: We started this project to save people's time and help them. If we were trying to steal your account, we wouldn't have bothered with the bot for so long and the bot wouldn't be verified. In addition, we are limited in what we can do with your account. For example, we cannot change your password or turn off 2-Step Verification.

How is my data protected?
